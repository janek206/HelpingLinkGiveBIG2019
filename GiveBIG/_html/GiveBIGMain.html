    How does GiveBIG work?

    GiveBig is a one-day, online charitable giving event organized by the 501 Commons to inspire people to give generously to nonprofit organizations who make the Greater Seattle Area a stronger, more vibrant community for all.

    For every donation made on May 8th, Helping Link will receive additional matching funds from 501 Commons.
